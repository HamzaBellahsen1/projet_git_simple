Welcome to Hamza Bellahsen's Restaurant

Experience the best of Moroccan cuisine!

Our Menu

Appetizers

  • Harira - Traditional Moroccan soup made with tomatoes, lentils, and spices.
  • Brik - Crispy pastry filled with egg and herbs, served with lemon.
  • Mezze Platter - A selection of Moroccan dips, olives, and bread.

Main Courses

  • Tagine Lamb - Slow-cooked lamb with apricots and almonds in a traditional tagine.
  • Couscous Royale - Steamed couscous served with a variety of meats and vegetables.
  • Chicken Pastilla - A savory-sweet pie filled with spiced chicken, almonds, and cinnamon.

Desserts

  • Baklava - Layers of phyllo pastry filled with nuts and honey.
  • Orange Salad - Fresh oranges with cinnamon and a drizzle of honey.
  • Mint Tea - Traditional Moroccan mint tea served with sugar.

About Us

Hamza Bellahsen's Restaurant is dedicated to providing a unique dining experience that celebrates the flavors of Morocco. Our chefs use the freshest ingredients to create traditional dishes that are both authentic and delicious.
